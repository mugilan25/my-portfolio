About Me

Founder. Engineer. Innovator.

I'm Mugilan S, an Electronics & Communication Engineering student and startup founder with hands-on experience in embedded systems, IoT and applied engineering solutions.

As Founder & Lead Engineer at AWLRI Group of Research, I design and develop hardware-software systems for industrial and defence-oriented applications — including air purification, smart agriculture, and waste-to-energy systems built around ESP32, sensors and real-time automation.

I also collaborate with Rudram Dynamics Foundation on defence-related prototype development, and I'm affiliated with the Atal Innovation Mission, NITI Aayog as a Community Innovation Fellow.

Ventures: Wake Edison Edutech · Rawlene Organic.
